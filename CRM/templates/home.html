About CRMS

Classroom Management System is a software application that helps teachers to manage and organize their classroom activities. This Idea was given by Mr.Jayapal Sir who is the CSE faculty in GITAM University. This is the application where the faculty can quickly see the availability of the class rooms. And also the conflicts that can be arised from the current timetable with the rooms can also be detected easily.
